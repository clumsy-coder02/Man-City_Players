Display English Premier League players based on team and position 

ManCity players

Each Team is a dictionary
inside the dictionary of a team there are also dictionaries(based on position) with,
list of players.

Team names are variables
positions are keys
players in that position are values(made as a list)

Positions:
1.GoalKeeping(dictionary)
    1.GoalKeeper(key)
        values(list, players)

2.Defense(dictionary)
    1.Right Back(key)
        values(list, players)
    2.Left Back(key)
        values(list, players)
    3.Center Back(key)
        values(list, players)

3.Midfield(dictionary)
    1.Defensive Midfielder(key)
        values(list, players)
    2.Central Midfielder(key)
        values(list, players)
    3.Attacking Midfielder(key)
        values(list, players)

4.Attack(dictionary)
    1.Center Forward(key)
        values(list, players)
    2.Left Winger(key)
        values(list, players)
    3.Right Winger(key)
        values(list, players)
